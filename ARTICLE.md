# PokéMMO: A Realtime Monster-Catching World With a Real On-Chain Economy

PokéMMO is a browser-based, realtime multiplayer adventure that fuses the nostalgia of classic monster-catching games with a living, on-chain economy built on Solana. There is nothing to download and no launcher to install — you simply open [pokemmo.io](https://pokemmo.io), create your trainer, and step straight into a shared pixel-art overworld where every other character you pass is a real person playing in the same instant. From the very first moment, the game is designed to feel familiar to anyone who grew up wandering routes and tall grass, while quietly introducing a token-powered layer that turns time spent in the world into something with real, tradeable weight.

At its heart, PokéMMO is about exploration and progression. You choose from fourteen distinct trainer sprites and an outfit tint to make your character your own, then set out across five connected places — from Pallet Town through winding routes to bustling Crysthaven City — either on foot or via instant fast-travel. Each route hides its own species of wild creatures in the grass; catch them with Poké Balls to earn experience and gold, level up to grow your stats, and build out a personal collection. Towns are alive with shopkeeper NPCs who sell items, forge weapons, breed rare companions, and heal your team, giving every settlement a reason to visit and a gameplay loop that rewards curiosity as much as grinding.

What sets PokéMMO apart is that its economy runs on real Solana, not just numbers in a database. Players can top up in-game Gold by sending SOL straight to the project treasury through their own wallet — every purchase is a genuine, wallet-signed mainnet transaction, verifiable on-chain, with no custody of keys by the game itself. That Gold then flows back into the world as Poké Balls, potions, weapons, and even rare Pokémon bought from breeders, creating a closed loop where on-chain value translates directly into in-game power and collection. The native **$POKE** token anchors the ecosystem, and the entire economy is transparent, configurable, and tied to a single public treasury.

Beyond spending, PokéMMO lets you put your SOL to work. A dedicated staking feature lets players lock SOL into the treasury and earn passive **$GOLD** yield that accrues continuously, claimable any time — a simple, honest way to reward long-term supporters of the project. Layered on top is a full story quest, "The Lost Treasure," where Prof. Oak, a riddling hermit, and an old sailor send you on a hunt across the region through branching dialogue and real choices. Clues you collect fill a journal and reveal glittering dig sites scattered through the world, each one paying out gold, rare gear, and the next clue — culminating in a legendary reward for the trainers who see it through.

PokéMMO is, above all, a social world. Avatars, positions, levels, and chat all sync in realtime, so you genuinely meet, talk to, and adventure alongside other players, and the experience is built to work just as smoothly on a phone — with a virtual joystick, auto-landscape, and a streamlined HUD — as it does on desktop.
